# Corpus Provenance and Attribution

## What this corpus contains

This release contains page-level OCR derived from the six Bedjan-Brock
volumes of Jacob of Serugh used in this project. Source scans and prepared
page images are not included in the present text-only release.

## Attribution

The source volumes are public-domain printed editions. The transcription was
generated independently with the Qoruyo models through Kraken. When reusing
the OCR, retain its volume and page identifiers, identify the source edition,
and cite the Qoruyo model records used for segmentation and recognition.

The OCR is a derived machine transcription rather than a new critical
edition and may contain recognition, segmentation, or ordering errors.
