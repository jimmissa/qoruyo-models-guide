# Corpus Provenance and Attribution

## What this corpus contains

The corpus contains page images and OCR derived from a 1903 printed edition of
Isaac of Antioch. The original and prepared images are retained to make the
OCR workflow auditable; the transcription files are derived outputs.

## Attribution

The corpus is derived from the 1903 Isaac of Antioch edition and is supplied as
public-domain source material and derived OCR. Please retain the page numbers,
cite the source edition, and identify the Qoruyo models used when reusing the
images or transcriptions.

The OCR is a derived transcription rather than a new critical edition.
